Purpose of the Presentation

This comprehensive data overview examines student population trends, school capacity, and disaggregated academic performance at Druid Hills High School. Our analysis provides critical insights into enrollment patterns, demographic shifts, and Biology EOC achievement gaps to inform strategic decision-making and resource allocation for student success.

Enrollment Analysis

Determine enrollment stability and assess school capacity utilization across grade levels and demographic subgroups.

Demographic Trends

Disaggregate enrollment by race/ethnicity, Students with Disabilities (SWD), English Language Learners (ELL), and Free/Reduced Lunch (FRL) status.

Biology EOC Performance

Identify patterns in academic underperformance on the Biology End-of-Course exam, disaggregated by race/ethnicity, SWD, ELL, and FRL over the last four years.

Achievement Gaps

Examine four-year trends in CCRPI and Biology EOC data to surface achievement gaps by student subgroup and identify equity concerns.

Intervention Planning

Provide actionable insights identifying which student groups need targeted intervention and support strategies.

Equity Alignment

Align school-level equity efforts with instructional planning and strategic resource allocation for maximum impact.
